About Me

I'm Eloísa, a Technical Audio & Sound Designer with hands-on experience in game audio implementation and audio postproduction for film. I've developed expertise in production sound, ADR recording, dialogue editing, SFX editing, and mixing in both stereo and 5.1 formats.

My experience extends beyond traditional post-production, having worked as part of technical crews for concerts, theater, and festivals. This diverse background has shaped my perspective on sound in live and interactive environments, giving me a unique understanding of how audio works across different mediums.

I'm passionate about immersive sound experiences and constantly exploring new creative challenges in interactive audio. My technical skills include proficiency in Pro Tools (Avid Certified User), audio middleware like Wwise and Fmod, and game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine.

In my free time, I enjoy playing instruments like guitar, piano, and drums, as well as lettering and gaming, which helps me stay connected to different forms of artistic expression and storytelling.
